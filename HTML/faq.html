Frequently Asked Questions

Please find below a list of our most frequently asked questions and their respective answers! Have a question that isn't here? Please contact us!

How do I become a member?

show more

Present yourself at the centre during business hours. Monday to Friday between 9 am and 4:30 pm. You will need to fill out a New Membership Form pay your membership and then purchase a punch pass for your fitness classes.

How much do memberships cost?

show more

Membership is $30.00 per year plus applicable taxes starting January 2023.

Activities & Events

show more

Some activities are free all Fitness classes run on a punch pass (see description below). Some activities like Creative Art Program do have a small fee to cover supplies. Fees are always advised ahead of time. ACTIVITIES FOR MEMBERS We have many fitness classes such as: Cardio Yoga Line dancing – Social Activities include: Fiddling Flat Picking JAM Session Card Making ACTIVITIES FOR MEMBERS AND NON-MEMBERS Lunch & Learns Education Art Classes Astronomy Classes and so much more INTERGENERATIONAL ACTIVITIES Take place on Saturdays and include: dance classes ZUMBINI art camp Punch Passes – 10 classes – $35.00 + HST – 23 classes – $70.00 + HST – MONTHLY – $50.00 + HST

Cancelations & Closures

show more

We will email everyone about cancelled classes or if the centre is closed post on our Facebook page. We also have an information line where we will have a recording stating any cancellation or closure. The center runs with the school system calendar we are closed for two weeks during the holiday and if they are closed because of a weather system we are also closed. The safety of our staff and members is essential to us.
